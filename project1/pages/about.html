About the project!

The 3D shopping web page project created by students in the CG class of the Computer Science course at UFPel is an impressive example of using WebGL features to enhance the online shopping experience. The project is designed to allow users to explore virtual shops and interact with 3D product models.

The virtual shops are designed with detailed 3D models of the stored items. The user can click on a product to select it, view it in 3D, and even zoom in and out to inspect it further. There are also options for adding the item to the cart.

To enhance the user experience, the project includes several WebGL features, such as lighting and shadow effects, realistic textures, and smooth animations. The 3D models are rendered in real-time, making it easy to navigate and explore the virtual shops.

Overall, this 3D shopping web page project is an excellent example of how technology can be used to enhance online shopping experiences. The use of WebGL features adds a new level of immersion and interactivity, making it an exciting and engaging shopping experience for users..
